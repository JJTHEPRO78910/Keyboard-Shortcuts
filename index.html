Top Keyboard Shortcuts

So you want to level up your working skills. Increase performance skils or just speed the process up, well you came to the right place. Here are some useful keyboard shortcuts to use in everyday life and in text proccessors like Microsoft Word and Google Docs.

To print any page, do ctrl+p
To highlight all of the text, just simply do ctrl+a
to make the text bold, do ctrl+b
to copy and paste, do ctrl+c and ctrl+v
to open a tab and close it, do ctrl+t and ctrl+w
